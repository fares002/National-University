1. Project Scope

The Company designed, developed, and delivered a financial management system (frontend + backend) including: authentication, role-based access, payments & expenses modules, reporting dashboard (daily/monthly/yearly), PDF exports, currency rate management, and deployment guidance.

2. Deliverables
